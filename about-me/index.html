About me

Hello, my name is Kenta and I’m a Software Engineering student studying at the University of Alberta. My passion is programming and I have a strong interest in large scale infrastructure.

Previously, I worked at Demonware Inc. in Vancouver, BC as an intern on the Capacity Planning and Monitoring Team. I worked with Monitoring infrastructure that monitors some of the biggest Activision titles such as Call of Duty and Crash Bandicoot. I also work on improving Capacity Planning tools to help plan the build outs for future games.

Before that, I also interned at Health Canada where I was a support analyst. I also volunteer with the Engineering Student Society at the U of A where I maintain and build their web infrastructure.
